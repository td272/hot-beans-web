Our Story

Founded in 2016, Hot Beans Web started with a simple mission: to create exceptional web experiences that help businesses thrive in the digital age. Over the past decade, we've grown from a small startup to a respected web development agency, serving clients across the UK and Europe.

Today, we specialise in building modern web applications, e-commerce platforms, and custom digital solutions using cutting-edge technologies. Our portfolio includes work for startups, SMEs, and established enterprises across various industries.

As we continue to grow, we're committed to nurturing the next generation of web developers through our trainee program, offering opportunities for talented individuals to learn, grow, and make a real impact.
